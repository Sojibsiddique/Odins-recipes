beef Kalavuna

Beef Kala Bhuna (কালা ভুনা) is a rich, spicy, and deeply flavored Bangladeshi dry beef curry

that’s slow-cooked until the meat becomes tender and dark (almost black) in color

key Ingredienta

  • Beef (with fat & bones) – slow-cooked
  • Lots of onions – both raw and fried (beresta)
  • Ginger-garlic paste – strong and aromatic
  • Whole spices – bay leaves, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves
  • Ground spices – turmeric, chili, coriander, garam masala
  • Mustard oil – gives it a traditional punch
  • Green chilies – for hea
  • Optional: Yogurt or vinegar (for tenderness)

Key Cooking mathode

  1. Beef is first marinated with spices.
  2. Cooked on low flame for a long time (often 2–3 hours).
  3. As water dries out, it's “bhuna-fied” — meat is stirred constantly to get a dry, sticky, dark coating.
  4. Finished with ghee or mustard oil and fried onions
