Chiken Roast

Bangladeshi-style Chicken Roast (চিকেন রোস্ট)

— the rich, flavorful, slightly sweet dish served at weddings with polao or khichuri.

Ingredients

  • Whole Chicken – 1 medium (cut into 4 or 8 large pieces)
  • Onion – 2 large (finely sliced)
  • Ginger paste – 2 tbsp
  • Garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  • Yogurt – ½ cup
  • Green chilies – 4–5 (slit)
  • Bay leaves – 2
  • Cinnamon sticks – 2 (1-inch)
  • Cardamom – 3–4
  • Cloves – 3–4
  • Mace (জায়েত্রী) – 1 small piece (optional)
  • Nutmeg (জায়ফল) – 1 pinch (optional)
  • Red chili powder – ½ tsp (adjust to taste)
  • Turmeric powder – ¼ tsp
  • Coriander powder – 1 tsp
  • Garam masala – ½ tsp
  • Sugar – 1 tsp (for slight sweetness)
  • Salt – to taste
  • Oil – ½ cup (you can use part ghee for richness)
  • Kewra water or rose water – 1 tsp (for aroma)
  • Fried onions (beresta) – ½ cup (for garnish)

Cooking Steps

  1. Marinate the Chicken
  2. Fry the Onions
  3. Cook the Chicken
  4. Add Yogurt & Simmer
  5. Add kewra water or rose water.
