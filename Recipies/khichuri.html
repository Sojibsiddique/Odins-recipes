Khichuri

Khichuri (খিচুড়ি) is a traditional South Asian comfort food made by cooking rice and lentils together

often with spices, vegetables, or meat. It’s especially popular in Bangladesh, India, and Nepal

khichuri ingredients

  • Rice 1 cup (chinigura or polao rice works best)
  • Moong dal (yellow lentils) – 1 cup (lightly roasted for flavor)
  • Potatoes – 2 medium (cut into cubes)
  • Carrots – 1 (optional, diced)
  • Green peas – ½ cup (optional)
  • Onion – 2 medium (sliced)
  • Green chilies – 4–5 (to taste)
  • Bay leaves – 2
  • Cinnamon stick – 1 piece (1 inch)
  • Cardamom pods – 2–3
  • Cloves – 3–4
  • Ginger paste – 1 tsp
  • Garlic paste – 1 tsp

Process of cooking

  1. Roast the Dal (Moong)
  2. Prepare the Masala
  3. Add Dal and Rice
  4. Add Water
  5. Finish with Aroma
